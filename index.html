Welcome

This script will give you a list of all the posts you have made across the Stack Exchange Network

Usage

Get an access token, by clicking this link. You'll get something like:

https://stackexchange.com/oauth/login_success#access_token=asda89223***32nsd8())&expires=86399

Copy and paste the thing between access_token= and &, ie. asda89223***32nsd8()).
